Rationale 2

The primary goal of formatting images is to find the balance between the smallest file size and acceptable quality. These are my tactics to troubleshoot issues with image optimisation;

  • Choose the right format (PNG, JPG, SVG and GIF)
  • Compress quality vs size (use higher compression rate for smaller images and low compression rate for larger images)
  • Usage of Lossy and Lossless (know when to compress data or remove data from images)
  • Resizing images to scale (create several sizes to respond to various screen sizes)
